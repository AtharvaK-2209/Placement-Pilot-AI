A brand-new AI agent that analyzes HOW users execute their roadmap and
provides personalized coaching recommendations.

KEY DIFFERENTIATORS:
  • Behavioral Pattern Detection — Identifies execution habits
  • Interview Readiness Scoring — Estimates prep level (0-100)
  • Risk Assessment — Burnout & deadline risk indicators
  • Topic-Level Analysis — Strong/weak subject detection
  • Personalized Coaching — Specific, actionable recommendations
  • Motivational Intelligence — Data-driven encouragement

COMPLEMENTS GOAL HEALTH:
  • Goal Health asks: "Can you complete the roadmap?"
  • Execution Intelligence asks: "How well are you executing?"

INPUT (ExecutionIntelligenceInput):
  • Goal & context (deadline, difficulty, feasibility)
  • Roadmap progress (version, weeks, completion %)
  • Task execution (completed, missed, by type)
  • Behavioral signals (XP, streak, consistency)
  • Goal health integration
  • Weekly completion patterns
  • Topic strength/weakness detection

OUTPUT (ExecutionIntelligenceScore):
  • overallPerformance — High-level assessment
  • strengths[] — 2-4 strong topics
  • weaknesses[] — 2-4 topics needing attention
  • behaviourPatterns[] — 3-5 AI-detected habits
  • recommendations[] — 3-5 actionable coaching tips
  • burnoutRisk — low/medium/high
  • deadlineRisk — low/medium/high
  • interviewReadiness — 0-100 score
  • motivationalMessage — Personalized encouragement
  • confidence — 0-100 AI confidence

  • System prompt: ~380 tokens
  • User prompt: ~180-220 tokens
  • Total: ~560-600 tokens (within budget)
  • Temperature: 0.2 (deterministic)
  • Max output tokens: 1536
